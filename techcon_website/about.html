Our History

TechCon began in 2018 as a small gathering of local tech enthusiasts in a university auditorium. Born from a desire to foster innovation and collaboration, its inaugural year focused on emerging AI trends. Over the years, it has grown exponentially, attracting international speakers and participants, becoming a pivotal event for thought leadership in the technology sector.

Each year has seen a significant increase in attendance and the diversity of topics covered, from blockchain and cybersecurity to sustainable tech and ethical AI. We pride ourselves on being a platform where groundbreaking ideas are shared and future technologies are shaped.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to empower the global tech community by providing an accessible and inclusive platform for knowledge exchange, networking, and innovation. We aim to:

  • **Inspire:** Showcase groundbreaking technologies and visionary ideas.
  • **Connect:** Facilitate meaningful interactions between professionals, researchers, and students.
  • **Educate:** Offer practical insights and hands-on experiences through workshops and presentations.
  • **Advance:** Contribute to the progress of the tech industry by highlighting future trends and challenges.

Notable Past Speakers

We've been privileged to host some of the brightest minds in technology. Here are a few notable past speakers who have graced the TechCon stage:

Dr. Anya Sharma (AI Ethicist)

Dr. Sharma is a leading expert in ethical AI development, advocating for responsible and unbiased machine learning. Her keynote at TechCon 2021, "AI for Good: Building a Fair Digital Future," challenged developers to prioritize societal impact in their work.

Mr. Kenji Tanaka (Quantum Computing Pioneer)

Mr. Tanaka is renowned for his pioneering work in quantum algorithm development. At TechCon 2022, he demystified quantum computing, explaining its potential to revolutionize industries from medicine to finance in his talk, "Beyond Bits: The Quantum Leap."

Ms. Chloe Davis (Cybersecurity Strategist)

Ms. Davis, a prominent cybersecurity strategist, shared critical insights on protecting digital infrastructure at TechCon 2023. Her session, "Fortifying Our Digital Frontiers: A New Era of Cyber Defense," highlighted the evolving threats and innovative solutions in the cybersecurity landscape.
